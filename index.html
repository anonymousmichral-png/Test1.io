Discover Our Mission

Why Biofoam?

A natural packaging revolution born from the urgent need to replace petroleum‑based styrofoam that chokes our planet.

What does Biofoam define?

Biofoam is a 100% home‑compostable packaging material with the same protective qualities as styrofoam – but made entirely from plants and agricultural waste. It naturally decomposes within weeks, not centuries.

Why is it needed?

Over 14 million tons of plastic end up in oceans yearly. Styrofoam (EPS) takes 500+ years to degrade, breaking into microplastics that enter our food chain. Biofoam offers a circular, non‑toxic end‑of‑life.

Target & Purpose

Designed for eco‑conscious businesses: electronics packaging, food delivery containers, fragile item shippers. We aim to replace single‑use protective foam in e‑commerce and retail by 2030.

Biological Foundation

Biofoam is alive – literally. Our material grows from four natural ingredients, bound by mycelium, the root network of fungi.

Water Hyacinth

Invasive aquatic plant, harvested to restore waterways. Its fibrous structure gives Biofoam cushioning strength.

Mycelium

Fungi root network acts as a natural glue, binding plant matter without chemicals into a solid foam‑like matrix.

Beeswax

Natural water‑resistant coating that repels moisture without plastic, yet remains fully biodegradable.

Rice Husk

Abundant agricultural byproduct adds rigidity and texture, replacing synthetic fillers completely.
